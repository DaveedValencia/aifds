Ecommerce Schema Profile

Draft schemas and examples for ecommerce implementations. Each artifact aligns with the governance rules in the AIFDS specification and can be copied directly from this page.

Profile Overview

The ecommerce profile standardizes JSON-LD for core storefront surfaces. It focuses on identity, navigation, and freshness so AI systems can trust how your catalogue is represented.

Current Coverage

  • Homepage: Organization/LocalBusiness, WebSite, and WebPage graph with search, breadcrumb, and trust signals.

Planned Additions

  • Collections / listing pages
  • Product detail pages

Severity What the validator checks
Critical JSON-LD block with `@context`, single Organization/WebSite/WebPage triad, shared HTTPS origin, and at least one contact channel with email or phone.
Strong Language tags, on-site SearchAction, verified profiles, mainEntityOfPage, logo dimensions, legal identity, and complete contact details.
Informational Breadcrumb trail, copyright signals, LocalBusiness address/hours, and high-quality topical context (`knowsAbout`).
