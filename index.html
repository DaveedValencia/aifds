Schemas that help AI truly know your business.

AIFDS is the open-source standard that extends schema.org for the AI era. We publish transparent schemas and documentation so organizations can ship trusted data and become the source AI systems trust by default.

Validator (Coming Soon)

Run your URL, receive a transparent report—graded Bad, Average, Good, or Excellent. The validator checks schema conformance, detects conflicts, and points you to the documentation that explains every finding.

  • No scoring vanity metrics, just clear pass/fail rationale.
  • Exportable reports for teams, partners, and clients.
  • Built to reinforce open standards, not lock you into a platform.
