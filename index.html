Mission

Make the web machine-interpretable, trustworthy, and contextually complete so AI systems can understand and recommend businesses accurately.

Vision

A governing framework for structured data integrity in the AI era—anchored in openly defined rules, transparent scoring, and measurable improvement.

The Problem

AI search and discovery rely on structured signals, yet most sites ship inconsistent, fragmented schema.org data, forcing models to guess.

The Gap

Legacy validators stop at syntax. They rarely check whether structured data reflects truth, leaving conflicts and contradictions unresolved.

The Impact

AIFDS delivers a cleaner, higher-trust data layer so businesses are represented accurately, developers get deterministic feedback, and AI systems source the facts they need.

Establish Standards

  • Extends schema.org with rule sets for identity, product/service detail, policies, navigation, and freshness.
  • Defines what “AI-ready structure” means across canonical signals and evidence.

Measure Quality

  • Grades structured data (Bad → Excellent) across Identity & Trust, Completeness, Navigation, and Freshness.
  • Pairs each score with a rubric so teams know what to fix, not just what failed.

Repair Conflicts

  • Detects duplicate or contradictory JSON-LD blocks and flags missing schema elements.
  • Suggests canonical corrections and evidence links, outputting human and machine-readable reports.

Enable AI Visibility

  • Enforces consistency so AI systems retrieve the right brand context.
  • Reduces hallucinations and elevates discoverability across AI-driven experiences.

Quality Scorecard

Scores range from Bad to Excellent, guiding remediation instead of issuing pass/fail verdicts.

Dimension Focus Outcome
Identity & Trust Canonical organization, contact, and ownership signals Verified brand authority
Structured Data Completeness Required schema fields and canonical consistency Machine-ready business facts
Navigation & Breadcrumbs Canonical hierarchy and discoverable pathways Interpretable site structure
Freshness & Evidence Timestamps, update trails, and corroborating links Current, verifiable data

Canonical About

AIFDS (AI Feed Discovery Standard) is an open standard for structured data governance. It defines how to validate and repair schema.org so that websites are accurately interpreted by search engines and large language models. AIFDS bridges the gap between SEO structure and AI reasoning—providing a shared language for technical accuracy, business context, and machine trust.
