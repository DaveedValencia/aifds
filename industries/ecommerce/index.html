Templates for every commerce moment

Use these templates together to guide buyers from discovery to purchase, with clear evidence at every step.

Template Description
Homepage Introduces the brand, value propositions, featured categories, promotions, and primary calls-to-action.
Collections Organizes products into navigable groupings complete with merchandising strategy and hero offers.
Product Pages Captures high-intent details—inventory, pricing, reviews, policies—so agents can answer the toughest PDP questions.
Blog Posts Bridges content marketing with commerce, pairing storytelling with shoppable modules and SEO signals.
Static Pages Standardizes policies, FAQs, support content, and credibility claims for consistency across your site.

How the templates work together

Connect the evidence threads agents rely on to move buyers forward without ambiguity.

Trust
